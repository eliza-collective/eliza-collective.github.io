ELIZA is a network of artists and technologists who explore the complex ways in which AI works, and is percieved by people.

Inspired by the ELIZA Effect, we explore how perceptions of agency in AI systems gives rise to new artistic questions and dilemmas. Our work has interogated who should get credit for AI-generated art, and how to improve people's notions of AI.
