ELIZA is a AI Art Collective that meditates on and reifies the social impact of artificial intelligence.

We are a team of artists, machine learning researchers and philosophers who believe AI needs to be better understood and conceptualized in order to be best used by society.

Inspired by the ELIZA effect, we use various mediums and methods to interrogate the agency we endow to our systems. But we aren't too serious about it.
